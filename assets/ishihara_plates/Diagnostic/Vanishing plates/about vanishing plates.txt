Vanishing Plate:
Purpose: To confirm red–green color vision deficiency.
Normal color vision: Sees the number clearly.
Color vision deficient: Cannot see the number.
User recognition: The number disappears for color-deficient users.
Diagnosis instruction: Failure to see the number on multiple vanishing plates confirms red–green color vision deficiency.
Used to detect: Protanopia, protanomaly, deuteranopia, deuteranomaly.
Diagnostic value: Yes.
